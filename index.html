About me

Who I am

I'm a final-year Information Technology (Honours) student at Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia (UTHM), passionate about building full-stack apps and crafting compelling digital experiences.

My capstone project, KimieBundle Ez-Shop, is a dual-platform e-commerce system for secondhand footwear — a React Native mobile app + React.js admin panel, both powered by Firebase. It achieved 95.5% UAT satisfaction across 22 real users.

Outside of code, I run KimieBundle, a preloved footwear reselling business across Malaysia, and I produce video content for events and social media. I'm also a JDT football fan, an FC26 player, and a Minecraft modding enthusiast.

Currently preparing for my industrial training at Mechanikos Sdn Bhd (Cyberjaya) — starting July 2026 — where I'll be working in the Technology Department.
